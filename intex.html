Here's some factors which might be
affecting your sleep:

Here's what you can do about it ...

Have a good bedtime routine

  • Leave at least 30 minutes to wind down before sleeping.
  • Try some relaxing activities before you sleep.
  • Listen to calm music.
  • Sleep and wake up at the same time everyday.
  • Sleep for more than 7 hours per night.

Have a good sleep environment

  • Make sure your room is dark or use a sleep mask.
  • Set a cool and comfortable temperature.
  • Consider a white noise machine or use the sound of a fan.
  • Use comfortable bedding.
  • Try sleep sprays which are calming and relaxing.

Reduce blue lights

  • Avoid blue light before sleep as it can obstruct melatonin production, a hormone that facilitates sleep.
  • Unplug electronics 30min before sleep to prevent mental stimulation.
